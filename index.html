Research
BSO - based public transport scheduling bilevel programming model and learning strategy optimization algorithm
(Thesis Being Delivered)
In this paper, we put forward with the aim to upper passenger satisfaction, satisfaction with the bus company for the bi-level programming model for the bottom of the target, solved by the model design BSO brainstorming optimization algorithm, and finally a city bus line as the instance, all-day bus operating conditions for the numerical simulation. It is verified that the proposed bilevel programming model and the designed BSO algorithm are effective and feasible.
Study on the regional differentiation of sponge city key technologies
Water pollution and treatment [J]. 2017, 5(2): 22-32.
This paper analyzes the impact of geographic differences, meteorological conditions and surface characteristics of matrix sponge and other factors on the city construction, and expounds the application effect of the key technology, preliminary definition of the key technology of sponge city regional differentiation, enrich and improve the sponge city theory to a certain extent.
